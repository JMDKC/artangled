Artangled is a bare-bones blog about art shows that lead to connections. It's written by [Joseph Clift](https://josephclift.com).

It is updated weekly, circumstances permitting.

**Colophon**
This site was made with [Gatsby.js](https://www.gatsbyjs.org/). It uses a very lightly modified [Minimal Blog Theme](https://github.com/LekoArts/gatsby-starter-minimal-blog) by [LekoArts](https://github.com/jmdkc/artangled). You can look at the site’s code [here](https://github.com/JMDKC/artangled).
